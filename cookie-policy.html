Cookie and Privacy Notice

Last updated: 1 May 2026

Summary

Danish Vocabulary Trainer uses optional Google Analytics cookies only if you choose Accept analytics in the cookie banner. If you choose Decline, Google Analytics is not started.

You can change your choice at any time by returning to the trainer and selecting Cookie settings in the footer.

Essential local storage

The site stores your cookie choice in your browser so the banner does not appear on every visit.

Name Purpose Provider Duration
danishVocabAnalyticsConsent Remembers whether you accepted or declined analytics. This website Until you clear browser site data.

Optional analytics cookies

If you accept analytics, this site loads Google Analytics to understand visits and improve the learning experience. Google Analytics is configured with IP anonymization and consent mode.

Name Purpose Provider Typical duration
_ga Helps distinguish visits for analytics reporting. Google Analytics Up to 2 years.
_ga_* Stores analytics session state for this Google Analytics property. Google Analytics Up to 2 years.

What data may be processed

Analytics may process information such as visited pages, approximate location, device and browser information, referrer information, and interaction events. The site does not intentionally send names, email addresses, quiz answers, or account information to Google Analytics.

How to withdraw consent

Open the trainer, select Cookie settings in the footer, and choose Decline. The site will disable analytics consent and try to remove existing Google Analytics cookies for this domain.

You can also clear cookies and site data in your browser settings.

Third-party provider

Google Analytics is provided by Google. Google may process analytics data according to its own terms and privacy documentation.
